How It Works

Here's how the process works from start to finish:

  1. Contact me: At the bottom of the page there is a contact form, fill it out and I'll get back to you.
  2. Consultation (Optional): I'll provide advice on materials and design modifications.

Estimated timeframes vary, but I'll keep you updated throughout the process.
